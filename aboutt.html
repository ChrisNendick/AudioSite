What is the purpose of this site?

This website was created to help animators easily find unique audio samples for practicing character animation. Animators often struggle to find good audio for lip syncing, so I built this site to solve that problem :)

Is this Safe?

Yes! This site is completely safe. No malware, viruses, or malicious files.. just MP4s. There's also no ads to avoid any disruptions.

How do I download audio?

There should be 3 dots next to the mp3 player on the home page. Click the dots and press download.

Does this work on Mobile?

As of now, you can still access the audio player on mobile, but you cannot directly download from it.

How can I support this site?
